Privacy policy

This site is intended for public information and direct email contact. We do not operate tracking scripts or user databases.

Information we receive

If you contact EII by email, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your name, organisation, email address and enquiry.

How information is used

Contact information is used to respond to enquiries, discuss services and manage legitimate education, partnership or administrative communication.

Website analytics and forms

This static site does not include server-side forms, account login, tracking scripts or a database. Your email client handles messages sent through mail links. No cookies are placed beyond those required for basic site operation.

Third-party services

The site loads no external analytics, advertising or social tracking scripts. Any links to external resources open in the same or a new tab without sending personal data.
