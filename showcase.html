Showcase catalogue

Scoping-ready offers for schools, groups and funders.

Each offer has a defined audience, timeline, outcomes and next steps. The aim is to make a pilot conversation concrete enough for leadership, safeguarding, operations and governance review.

Schools and district leaders

Pilot-ready

School AI readiness sprint

A focused advisory sprint to align classroom AI adoption with safeguarding, pedagogy and compliance requirements.

Timeline: 4 weeks

Discovery: EUR 8k-15k

Pilot: EUR 25k-60k

Scale: EUR 120k+/year

  • Baseline AI usage map across staff and learner workflows.
  • Risk register with policy-level mitigations.
  • 90-day rollout plan for trustworthy AI literacy.

Next steps: nominate safeguarding and pedagogy leads, approve baseline data collection, and confirm the board-ready decision checkpoint.

Teacher teams and training partners

In pilot

Educator capability lab

A hands-on professional development model that pairs practical AI tools with lesson design guardrails and implementation evidence.

Timeline: 6-8 weeks

Discovery: EUR 10k-18k

Pilot: EUR 35k-80k

Scale: EUR 150k+/year

  • Facilitated workshop sequence with reusable lesson templates.
  • Teacher confidence and implementation scorecards.
  • Institution-ready evidence pack for funders or boards.

Next steps: select a teacher cohort, define the lesson-design baseline and commit to an evidence review after the first workshops.

Investors, foundations and strategic clients

Production candidate

Impact diligence pack

A diligence-ready package that translates programme delivery into governance, outcomes and operational confidence signals.

Timeline: 2-3 weeks

Discovery: EUR 6k-12k

Pilot: EUR 20k-45k

Scale: EUR 90k+/year

  • Claims register mapped to source evidence.

Multi-school groups, districts and regulators

Concept

AI safeguarding control centre

An operational cockpit prototype for policy controls, risk scoring and incident-response readiness across AI-enabled learning workflows.

Timeline: MVP in 2 weeks

Discovery: EUR 12k-20k

Pilot: EUR 45k-95k

Scale: EUR 180k+/year

  • Policy checklist with accountable owners.
  • Composite risk score with actionable thresholds.
  • Exportable governance summary for board or regulator review.

Next steps: finalise control taxonomy, integrate incident workflow feeds and approve governance export format.
