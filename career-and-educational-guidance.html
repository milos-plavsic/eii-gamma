Guidance across life stages

Clearer education and career decisions at every stage.

Guidance governance

Structured, practical and responsible.

EII guidance programmes focus on self-awareness, opportunity awareness, decision-making and transition skills, with proportionate GDPR-aware handling of learner information.

  • Age-appropriate learner activities and reflection tools.
  • Support for families, teachers and education leaders.
  • Clear next steps that connect learning to future pathways.
  • Guidance notes and learner data handled on a need-to-know basis, with retention and consent practices agreed before delivery.

Institutional support

Guidance programmes that can be adopted by schools and organisations.

Framework and materials

Age-specific activities, reflection prompts, pathway maps and decision tools that staff can use consistently.

Staff and family alignment

Shared language and practical resources for teachers, parents and guardians supporting learner choices.

GDPR-aware delivery

Clear handling of sensitive learner context, consent expectations, access boundaries and record-keeping instructions.
