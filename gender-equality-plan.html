Commitment

Gender equality plan

EII supports inclusive, respectful and equitable participation in education and organisational activity.

Purpose

This plan states EII's commitment to gender equality in learning opportunities, partnerships, communications and organisational practice.

Priorities

  • Promote equal access to educational guidance, workshops and learning opportunities.
  • Use inclusive language and representative materials wherever practical.
  • Consider gender equality in programme design, participation and review.
  • Respond seriously to concerns about discrimination, harassment or exclusion.

Review

The plan is reviewed as programmes, partnerships and organisational responsibilities evolve. Questions or concerns may be directed to office@eii.edu.eu.
