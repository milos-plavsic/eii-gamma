Trustworthy AI support for schools and education leaders.

AI can strengthen learning when it is adopted with clear purpose, governance, safeguarding and human accountability.

AI readiness assessment

Map current AI use, identify risks and define a realistic adoption plan for staff and learners.

Responsible AI policy

Ethical AI audits

Review tools, workflows and governance assumptions before AI systems become embedded in daily learning.

Educator training

Help teachers use AI tools productively while preserving professional judgement and learner agency.

Leadership advisory

Support boards and senior leaders with decision checkpoints, risk registers and implementation plans.

Safeguarding by design

Place learner welfare, transparency and escalation routes at the centre of any AI-enabled education workflow.

Governance that educators can actually use.

EII focuses on practical controls, not abstract principles alone. The aim is to help institutions move from uncertainty to controlled implementation.

  • Baseline AI usage map across staff and learner workflows.
  • Risk register with policy-level mitigations.
  • Board-ready rollout plan for trustworthy AI literacy.
  • GDPR-aware instructions for prompts, personal data, records, retention and human review.
