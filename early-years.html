Career and educational guidance

Early years

Early guidance builds curiosity, confidence and social learning through play, language and discovery.

Strong foundations for later choices.

At this stage, guidance is not about fixed career choices. It is about helping children explore, communicate and understand themselves and others.

  • Curiosity and problem-solving activities.
  • Confidence, cooperation and communication.
  • Gentle links between interests, skills and the wider world.
